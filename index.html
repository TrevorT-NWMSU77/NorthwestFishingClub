Who Can Join The Club?

Any current Northwest student who wants to learn more about fishing as a whole and to have a great time with great people with similar interests.

What Are the Requirements to Join the Club?

The only requirement to join the club is the annual club dues of $20. Once you pay your club dues, you will receive a free fishing club t-shirt.
*dues are paid every academic year, not semester*

Proud Sponsor

We have quite a few sponsors for out club but, one in particular stands out. The Leavenworth Bass Club have done a lot of things for us such as sending boats for those who don't have boats to compete in our Bearcat CLassic on a yearly basis. If you want to learn more about the LBC, check them out on their website.
